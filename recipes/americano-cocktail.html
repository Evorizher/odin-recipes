Americano Cocktail

Description

A simple recipe to make Americano Cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice
  • 1.5 fluid ounces bitter orange aperitif (such as CampariÂ®)
  • 1.5 fluid ounces sweet vermouth
  • 3 ounces club soda, or as needed
  • 1 (1 inch) piece orange peel

Steps

  1. Add ice to a tall glass. Pour in Campari and vermouth. Stir and top with soda water. Squeeze a twist of orange peel over the drink and drop it in.
