Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe to make lasagna, taken from https://www.alrecipes.com.

Making this as the first project of The Odin Project.

Ingredients

  • Meat: sweet Italiand sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: one onion and two cloves of garlic.
  • Tomato products: a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: two tablespoons.
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper.
  • Cheeses: parmesar, mozzarella, ricotta.
  • Egg: one egg.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
